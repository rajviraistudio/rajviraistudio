1. Information We Collect

When you use Raj Vir AI Studio (rajviraistudio.online), we may collect the following information:

  • Name and Email Address — when you subscribe to our newsletter, join our WhatsApp community, or sign up for course/tool access
  • Google Account Information — when you sign in with Google to access our Courses or Free Tools pages, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from Google
  • Usage Data — basic analytics such as pages visited, time spent, and device type (collected via Google Analytics if enabled)
  • Feedback & Ratings — star ratings you submit on newsletter editions

We do not collect payment information, passwords, phone numbers, or any sensitive personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Sending you our weekly AI newsletter (if subscribed)
  • Providing access to Courses and Free AI Tools sections
  • Understanding which content is most useful to our audience
  • Improving our website and newsletter based on feedback
  • Communicating important updates about Raj Vir AI Studio

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using the following services:

  • Supabase — stores subscriber names and emails with row-level security enabled
  • Firebase (Google) — manages authentication for Google Sign-In
  • Brevo — manages newsletter delivery

All services we use follow industry-standard security practices. Data is transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted connections) at all times.

Data Retention: We retain your information for as long as you are subscribed or have an active account. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at hello@rajviraistudio.online.

4. Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use the following types of cookies and tracking technologies:

  • Essential cookies — required for the website to function (e.g. login sessions)
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how visitors use our site (Google Analytics)
  • YouTube cookies — set when you watch embedded YouTube videos on our site

You can disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at hello@rajviraistudio.online. We will respond within 7 business days.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at hello@rajviraistudio.online and we will delete it promptly.
